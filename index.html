Welcome message

Overview of the Statistics Department

Philosophy:

To expose students to modern statistical theory and various applications of Statistics. Throughout the programme, there will be emphasis on laboratory-based work and problem-based learning via statistical inference, modelling, and programming, analyses and interpretation of real -life problems.

Vision Statement

To be the leading Statistics Department among Nigeria University, by offering high-quality statistical training and services, contributing to positive socio-economic transformation of the nation.

Mission Statement

To provide high-quality educational programmes through the adoption of different teaching methods and practical approaches in line with the community and labor market requirements.
